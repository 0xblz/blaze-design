I'm Blaze, a designer currently at PartyDAO working on UNO.

Previously, I was the founding designer at Showtime, and collaborated with WorkOS on their marketing site. Earlier in my career at Bitovi, I delivered major web projects for Lowe's and Levi's.

Before that, I worked as a freelance designer specializing in 3D projects, and at Scribd - where I helped design and refine their core website.

Recently I've built:

  • How's the Roads? - a fast way to check live traffic cameras and road conditions
  • Wind Vector - a 3D visualization of wind patterns across US states
  • Solar Flare - a Swift terminal app for monitoring solar activity and space weather
  • Weather - a Swift terminal app for current conditions, forecasts, and weather alerts
  • Holy Hues - for creating beautiful Scripture wallpapers with custom fonts, colors, and multiple translations
