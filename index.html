I'm Blaze, a product designer who recently led design at PartyDAO for UNO and ORO - apps on World Chain for verified humans on the World ID network. UNO is the #1 wallet for swapping, sending, and earning tokens, serving over 4 million users. ORO is a daily-claimable token with 3.8M users.

Previously, I was the founding designer at Showtime, where I built the product and design system for a web3 social network that raised $7.6M from Paradigm and others. I scaled the platform from concept to a cross-platform iOS, Android, and web application.

Before that, I collaborated with WorkOS - a $100M-funded API platform serving OpenAI, Cursor, Perplexity, and Vercel - on their marketing site and digital presence. Earlier at Bitovi, I delivered production web experiences for Fortune 500 brands including Lowe's and Levi's.

I've also worked at Scribd designing core web experiences, and as a freelance designer specializing in 3D visualization and interactive projects.

On the side, I ship iOS apps like How's the Roads? for checking live traffic cameras and road conditions.

I bring a unique combination of design leadership, systems thinking, and technical implementation - comfortable leading 0→1 products, scaling platforms to millions of users, and building production features end-to-end.
