@0xblz

designer

Since 2022, I've been part of PartyDAO, where I design UNO, the most widely used wallet on World. I've also contributed to the design of ORO, Diamante, and Party, a group chat with a shared wallet that can do anything onchain.

From 2021 to 2022, I was the founding designer at Showtime and collaborated with WorkOS on marketing graphics. Earlier in my career at Bitovi from 2015 to 2017, I delivered major web projects for Lowe's and Levi's. Between 2013 and 2017, I worked as a freelance designer focused on experimental 3D projects, including space and weather visualizations. Before that, from 2010 to 2012, I was at Scribd, designing and enhancing their core website.
