Experience

Since 2022, I've been part of PartyDAO, where I design UNO, the most widely used wallet on World. I've also contributed to the design of ORO, Diamante, and Party, a group chat with a shared wallet that can do anything on-chain.

From 2021 to 2022, I was the founding designer at Showtime and collaborated with WorkOS on marketing graphics. Earlier in my career at Bitovi, I delivered major web projects for Lowe's and Levi's. Before that, I worked as a freelance designer specializing in 3D projects, and at Scribd, where I helped design and refine their core website.
