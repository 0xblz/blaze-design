✴ about

I'm Blaze, a designer currently at PartyDAO working on UNO.

Previously, I was the founding designer at Showtime, and collaborated with WorkOS on their marketing site.

Earlier in my career at Bitovi, I delivered major web projects for Lowe's and Levi's.

Before that, I worked as a freelance designer specializing in 3D projects, and at Scribd - where I helped design and refine their core website.

I also build side projects - like How's the Roads?, an iOS app with live traffic camera feeds, Holy Hues, an app for creating Scripture wallpapers, and Wind Vector, a 3D visualization of wind patterns across US states.
